How to use Fast Facts Simulator

Quick reference: Settings, Modes, Hints, Timer, Scoring.

Filters & Controls

  • Category — choose a topic from the header dropdown; select Random to have categories chosen each round.
  • Timer — choose a countdown or set Timer: Off to disable timing for rounds.
  • Settings (gear) — contains Tournament, Show Scoreboard, Auto Advance, Hints, Dark/Light, and Reset Scores.

Modes

  • Tournament — single toggle in Settings. When enabled: filters are locked, category is forced Random, timer is set to 30s, Auto Advance is enabled, and hints are disabled.
  • Competitive behavior — hints are disabled in competitive/Tournament mode; scoring/readiness impacts are stronger.

Hints

  • Hints (Settings) — choose All, Half, or None. Half reveals up to half the canonical answer; the Hint button will disable once the cap is reached.
  • Give Up — reveals canonical and example answers for the round.

Round Flow & Input

  • A letter appears on the right and the category on the left. Type answers starting with that letter.
  • Live validation accepts correct answers as you type and shows a green checkmark; correct answers end the round and display examples.
  • After a round ends the Hint button becomes Next Round; press Enter to advance. Auto Advance will move after a short countdown if enabled.

Scoring & Persistence

  • Streak, Best, Correct count, and Rounds are shown in the scoreboard (toggle visibility in Settings).
  • Tournament Readiness (0–1000) is shown in the score panel and influences competitive scoring behavior.
  • Settings and filters are saved to localStorage under fastfacts_filters. Scores are saved under fastfacts_score.

Tip: Use the gear menu for all persistent preferences (mode, hints, auto-advance, scoreboard visibility, theme, reset).
